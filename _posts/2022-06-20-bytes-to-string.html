Sometimes in Golang (or any language), we often unintentionally print/debug an array of bytes as it is, instead of its string representation. You may have seen something like this:
 
error unmarshaling, body: [123 34 99 111 100 101 34 58 34 79 75 34 44 34 109 101 115 115 97 103 101 34 58 34 79 75 34 125]
 
If we want to quickly find out what it is, we can use this Python script:
 
python3 -c 'import re; print("".join([chr(int(x)) for x in re.findall("\d+", input("array of bytes: "))]))'
